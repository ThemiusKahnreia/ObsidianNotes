The Timeline for the campaign.

This takes place further into the story of [[Themius V Kahnreia]], and his impact on [[The World of Eden]] at large.

In 5327 H.A. ([[Heroic Age]]), Themius V Kahnreia, son of [[Titus V Kahnreia]], the Last Great King of Kahnreia, who died during [[The Fall of Ancient Kahnreia]], awakens to find himself a servant of a [[Lost Divinity]] named [[Astaroth]], the god of Space. Over the next 541 years, Themius takes his time in a journey to discover more about himself and learn about everything the world he lives in has to offer.
