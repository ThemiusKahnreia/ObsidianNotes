Warfare Incorporated is a **Semi-Modern Industrial Warfare TTRPG System**, set in a world based on the question of "*What if the cold war had no nuclear weapons as a deterrent?*"


The Players will participate, controlling their characters known both in meta and in world as **[[Operators]]**, Soldiers of a mercenary company of their own making.

This setting is full of these PMCs ([[Private Military Companies]]), hired all the time to support and provide proxy troops for combats all over the world. It's no rarity to see them, though most developed nations still have their own military that they would prefer to utilize or operate through if possible.

On many occasions, PMCs will make contracts with nations less developed, or as a deniability op with nations that are better developed. This isn't to say that PMCs don't appear in normal conflict between larger nations, but that it may be more rare in these instances.

The players can name their own PMC, and create an insignia for it if they so desire. They will increase in power largely through influence and increasing the military presence of their PMC.
