Specs are this TTRPG's "class system", and they provide players with extra ways to equip themselves and make their characters unique. 

When creating your [[Operators]], you will pick a Primary Spec and a Secondary Spec. They all come from the same list, the difference is how much you get from one individual Spec.

You get all perks from your Primary Spec, but only things in the "Core" section of your Secondary Spec.

---
## Specs in active duty:

- [[Frontliner]], the Breacher/Assaulter. Their job: get the team to the objective; walls, doors and blockades be damned. They are, as insinuated, the frontline fighter.
- [[Demolitionist]], the Engineer/Anti-Armor specialist. Their job: Remove all armored threats from the battlefield, and destroy any key objectives with high-payload explosives.
- [[Fire Support]], the Machine Gunner. Their job: Lay down heavy fire and provide suppression for the team to move tactically, or to simply stop the enemy from advancing.
- [[Medic]], the uh... Medic. Their job: keep teammates in the fight and provide logistical support.
- [[Scout]], the Reconnaissance Expert. Their job: keep mobile and support the team with accurate mid-range DMR fire and tactical spotting.
- [[Marksman]], the Sniper. Their job: Kill enemy H.V.T.s with precision and provide overwatch cover for teammates closer to the frontline.
- [[Commander]], the Tactician. Their job: boost morale of the team while calling in devastating support from the PMC's other personnel.


### A spec for the normal setting (at least):

- **==SECONDARY SPEC ONLY==** [[Amplifier]], the Radio Operator. Their job: While carting their primary gear, they keep near their team and help called-in support, and provide other equipment bonuses.
