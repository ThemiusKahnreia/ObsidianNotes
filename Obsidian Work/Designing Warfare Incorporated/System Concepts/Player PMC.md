The player PMC will have the ability to be expanded to accommodate acquisitions of new gear and other types of logistical and offensive support.  

The PMC base has constructable and upgradeable compartments, and in the base setting, is a small airbase that was abandoned for being partially destroyed before getting sold to the players.

The compartments are as follows:
- [[The Operations Room]], the default main hub. can be upgraded for an operation boost, and to unlock the upgrades for all the other rooms/buildings.
- [[The Armory]], a room for storing and doing maintenance on the firearms and weapons used by the Operators.
- [[Gadget R&D]], a building for developing and maintaining the gadgets and kit deployed by Operators of the PMC.
- [[The Hangar]], A building for storing the aircraft the PMC can call in and all of the equipment to maintain and service them.
- [[The Infirmary]], A wing of the main building dedicated to keeping all personnel of the PMC operating at full capacity.
