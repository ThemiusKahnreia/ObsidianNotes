The health and damage system in Warfare Incorporated is a system of [[Luck]] and [[Wounds]].

As mentioned in [[Operators]], these are very lucky people. So in combat, they have a certain amount of this [[Luck]] to burn through before they actually get shot and take [[Wounds]].

Beware of [[Massive Hits]], as they can cause complications to an operation quite quickly!

Some enemies can have [[Armor]], which defends them against many forms of attack, try and keep a piece of anti-armor equipment on you whenever you can!
