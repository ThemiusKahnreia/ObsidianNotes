Luck is the primary defense against incoming damage. Literally representing an Operator's 'luck', it kicks in whenever you get "hit" by an attack. The attack's damage will be dealt to the Operator's Luck, reducing it to a minimum of 0.

If luck is at 0, and the given Operator takes damage again, they take [[Wounds]] before their luck resets.

Luck is modified by your [[PRES]] stat, with the following equation:

8 + PRES = Operator luck.

it can be further modified by your [[Equipped Armor]], providing a bonus or detriment to your total luck.
