The Gadget R&D building has labs meant for gadget testing and facilities that prep them for requisition and keep them stored securely. Upgrading this can get you more gadgets, and possibly even upgrades to certain types of gadgets.

- **LVL 1 -** New gadgets available for unlock.
- **LVL 2 -** All gadgets gain +1 usage. More gadgets become unlockable.
- LVL 3 - All gadgets gain +1 usage back from resupply. More gadgets to unlock.
