This room provides storage and maintenance stations for any and all personnel weaponry. Some weapons require a certain level of Armory to obtain.

- **LVL 1 -** The armory can now be utilized for the storage and maintenance of weaponry. If a player character loses their weapon or it gets destroyed, they can purchase a new one here with group permissions.
- **LVL 2 -** The armory's increased level allows it to perform better maintenance and store larger or more sophisticated weaponry.
- **LVL 3 -** The armory, at max level, unlocks some of the more odd/specific weapons. Truly 1 of a kind in some cases.
