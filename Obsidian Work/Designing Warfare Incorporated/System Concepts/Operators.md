
Operators is the canon name of any given military personnel of a PMC, instead of a more general "Soldier".

Player **Operators** tend to be very lucky individuals, but by all accounts are simply human. This is accounted for in our [[Health and Damage System]]. 

To describe the training or understanding (as some people are naturals in a given field of work) of their equipment and specialties, the players will give their Operator a set of [[Specs]], or "Specializations". Spec is the canon in-world term for an Operator's field of specialization, and could reasonably be seen on a hiring profile.


They also have a set amount of slots for equipment they can carry into the fight. This is, assumedly, referred to generally as a "[[Loadout]]".

And of course, Player operators are expected to work as a team if they hope to survive the fighting that calls their name.

The [[Player PMC]] is ran by the players, but will have rules & limits set by the GM and their setting. Some PMC's may have more interactable NPCs, and others may have less, up to GM discretion.

Each Operator has a set of [[Stats]] that define their capabilities, both physical and psychological.
