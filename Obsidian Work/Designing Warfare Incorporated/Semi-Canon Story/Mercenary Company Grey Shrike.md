This mercenary company is a part of the [[Semi-Canon Story]], and is made up of 4 mercenaries who keep their names off of all official and unofficial records that even most handlers have access to.

In an interview with [[The Interviewer]], the Leader of Grey Shrike talks about all members of his company individually:
- [[Grey Shrike Commander]], the leader himself, who is surprisingly humble, and seems to have used to been a demolitionist before rising up and founding this mercenary company.
- [[Grey Shrike Frontliner]], the operator taking up the Frontliner Spec role in the company, being one of the more hardened operators of the group.
- [[Grey Shrike Medic]], or "The Newbie", as the commander referenced him, is the newest member of Grey Shrike, with combat medic and professional medical training, as well as the skills necessary to assist the commander as an "instigator"... whatever that means.
- [[Grey Shrike Marksman]], the keep-to-himself marksman spec operator who surprised the commander with intricate knowledge of Fire Support tactics.
